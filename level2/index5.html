                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   Went envying her and me -
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   Yes! - that was the reason .
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   That the wind came out of the cloud by night,
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   Chilling and killing my Annabel Lee.
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   But our love it was stronger by far than the love.
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   And neither the angels in heaven above,
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   Nor the demons down under the sea,
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   Can ever dissever my soul from the soul.
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   Of the beautiful Annabel Lee.
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   For the moon never beams without bringing me dreams,
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   And the stars never rise but I feel the bright eyes,
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   And so, all the night tide, I lie down by the side.
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   In her sepulchre there by the sea,
                                                                                    The angels, not half so                   In her tomb by the sounding sea.
